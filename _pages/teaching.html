Teaching is one of the most rewarding parts of my academic work. I enjoy seeing students approach mathematics with curiosity and develop confidence in their ability to solve problems. My teaching evolves alongside my students, and I continually refine my teaching philosophy and classroom practices in response to their needs.

I aim to create a supportive and rigorous learning environment in which students are encouraged to ask questions, explain their reasoning, and view difficulty as a natural part of learning mathematics. I believe that dedication, clear communication, and consistent effort help students build both mathematical understanding and resilience.
