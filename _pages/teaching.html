Teaching is one of the most rewarding experiences for me. I love to see my students explore math with curiosity and confidence. As a teacher, I am always willing to improve myself, develop my teaching philosophy, and refine my teaching skills outside the classroom. My teaching evolves alongside my students; in many ways, they teach me to be a better teacher.

I aim to create a supportive and rigorous learning environment in which students are encouraged to ask questions, explain their reasoning, and view challenge as a natural part of learning mathematics. I believe that dedication, clear communication, and consistent effort help students build both mathematical understanding and resilience.
